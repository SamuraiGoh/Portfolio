About Me

I am a photographer based in Minneapolis Minnesota and Eugene Oregon. I specialize in sports photography but do other subjects such as: nature, landscape, wildlife, and street photography. I have been doing photography for 5 years and have experience in photo editing, video editing, and camera and audio equipment. I have taken on many paying clients that involve sports and senior portraits and traveled across the country taking photos. With my photography style I like to be as creative as possible and highlight the art side of photography whether its through framing or capturing the right moment. Photography for me is a way to show people the world through my lens and appreciate a moment in time.

Goal

My goal is to serve my clients with high quality photos and edits while granting their request. It is also my mission to create art through the lens and provide a source of entertainment. My clients will receive artistic high quality photos that not only capture a subject but tell a story. I hope that other photographers can draw inspiration from my work and can provide feedback if needed. Below you can find a gallery of some of my work. Click on the boxes to look through my photos.
